Dashboard features

Here's what each section of your FitPulse dashboard does.

Metric cards
At-a-glance summary of calories, workouts, distance, and sleep — updated weekly.
Calories chart
Daily burn vs your goal as a bar chart. Bars turn teal when you hit the target.
Heart rate zones
Donut showing time split across Rest, Fat Burn, Cardio, and Peak zones.
Sleep tracker
Nightly sleep hours as a line chart — spot patterns and dips at a glance.
Activity breakdown
Progress bars per activity showing how close you are to weekly targets.
Week navigation
Use the arrows at the top to switch between weeks and review past performance.
